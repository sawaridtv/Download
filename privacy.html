Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sawarid (سواريد). Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your privacy when you use our media player application.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We believe in total privacy. We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Your Name, Email Address, or Phone Number
  • Your Exact GPS Location
  • Your Contacts or Files

No user registration or account creation is required to use Sawarid.

3. Local Storage & Playlists

Sawarid is purely a client-side media player. All the M3U playlists you add, your streaming history, and your favorite channels are stored strictly locally on your device's memory. We do not upload, sync, or monitor your private playlists on our servers.

4. Anonymous Analytics

To improve app performance, identify crashes, and enhance user experience, we use Firebase Analytics. It only tracks completely anonymous data, which includes:

  • Total number of active online users.
  • Unique device counts (using Android ID).
  • App crash reports and basic interactions.

It does not track what specific videos, channels, or content you are watching.

5. Third-Party Advertisements

To support our development and server costs, Sawarid displays advertisements from third-party ad networks (such as Adsterra). These networks may use cookies, your IP address, or advertising IDs to provide relevant ads. We do not control the data collected by these third-party services. Please interact with external ads at your own discretion.

6. Permissions We Request

The app may request certain permissions (like Storage or Notifications) solely for core functionalities like reading local videos, saving app updates, and displaying background radio controls. We do not misuse these permissions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and on this page.
